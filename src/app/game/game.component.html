Official Game Rules
You will have 3 options to play the game.

Option 1: Rock
++: Rock crushes Scissors
-- : Rock is covered by Paper

Option 2: Paper
++: Paper covers Rock
-- : Paper is cut by Scissors

Option 3: Scissors
++: Scissors cut Paper
-- : Scissors are crushed by Rock

Choose wisely...
